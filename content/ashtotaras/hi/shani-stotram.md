# Shani Stotram

Konastha Pingalo Babhruh Krishno Roudrontako Yamah  
Sauri Shaneshcharo Mandah Pippaladennamostute рее

<!--more-->

<br>

Nilanjana Samabhasam Raviputram Yamagrajam  
Chaya Martanda Sambhutam Tam Namami Shaishcharam рее

<br>

Om Sham Shanaishcharaya Namah рее
